    • Luffy D. Monkey


    Pirate emperor and captain of the straw hat pirates, Luffy ate the "gum-gum" fruit, making him rubbery and elastic.


    Evolution of the bounty

    As a pirate, he is higly wanted by the marine. His bounty increased as he pursued his adventure as a pirate. From his first bounty at 30,000,000 berries, he is currently wanted for 3,000,000,000 berries. It increased as followed :

    30,000 Berries
    First bounty. He received it after defeating some important pirate like Buggy and Arlong
    100,000,000 Berries
    The bounty went up after defeating the pirate warlord Crocodile on Alabasta
    300,000,000 Berries
    His victory againt CP9's Rob lucci tripled the bounty
    400,000,000 Berries
    Luffy's participation in the paramount war and because of his lineage with the leader of the Revolutionary army, Dragon increased the value of his bounty
    500,000,000 Berries
    The defeat of Don Flamingo in Dressrossa resulted in this increase
    1,500,000,000 Berries
    This massive change in the bounty is due to Luffy's rampage on Whole Cake island, Big Mom's territory
    3,000,000,000 Berries
    The straw hat and his alliance victory against two of the emperor of the sea, Big Mom and Kaido, doubled Luffy's bounty
